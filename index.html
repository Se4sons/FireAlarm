How it works
Three steps, fully paperless
No app installs, no accounts for tenants — just scan and sign.
1
Register your property
Add the property address and every alarm in the unit — bedroom smoke detector, hallway CO detector, kitchen heat detector, and more. Each unit gets one single QR code.
2
Print and attach the QR code
Print the QR code from the admin panel and stick it on or near the alarm panel or inside the unit door. Any tenant or inspector can scan it with their phone camera — no app needed.
3
Scan, test, and sign off
The inspection page opens instantly with step-by-step testing instructions for each alarm type. The inspector fills in the result, checks a replacement checklist, draws their signature, and submits.
Supported alarm types
Instructions for every alarm
The inspection form automatically shows the correct testing steps based on alarm type.
🔔
Smoke detector

💨
CO detector

🔴
Combo smoke/CO

🌡️
Heat detector

Features
Everything you need
Built for property managers with multiple units and tenants.
📱
One QR code per unit
Scan one code to inspect all alarms in a unit — bedroom, hallway, kitchen, and more. No separate codes to manage.
✍️
Digital signatures
Tenants and inspectors draw their signature directly on screen. Signatures are saved with each inspection record.
⚠️
Replacement flags
A built-in checklist flags alarms that are 10+ years old, damaged, or failing — so you know when to act.
📋
Full inspection log
Every sign-off is stored with the inspector name, role, result, notes, and signature. Export everything to CSV anytime.
🖨️
Print-ready QR codes
Print a single QR code or all properties at once. Each printed card includes the address and property ID.
📊
Dashboard overview
See at a glance how many properties are up to date and how many are due for inspection within the last 30 days.
